LuxeCare Laundry Lounge

Located on the 3th floor, our modern 3,000 square foot Laundry Facility delivers a truly elevated service experience — combining convenience, efficiency, and care. With expansive windows offering natural light and a calming view, it's more than just a utility space; it's a hub of cleanliness and comfort. Outfitted with cutting-edge commercial washers, dryers, and eco-friendly cleaning systems from globally trusted brands, we ensure your garments receive the highest level of care. Whether it’s a quick refresh, deep cleaning, or delicate fabric treatment, our facility is designed to handle all your laundry needs with precision and professionalism.

The Art of Luxury Fabric Care

Our luxury fabric care service is designed to protect and preserve the quality of delicate garments. From silks and cashmere to lace and fine wools, each item is carefully evaluated and treated using specialized techniques tailored to its fabric and construction. We use gentle handwashing, eco-friendly solvents, and low-heat steaming to ensure every piece retains its original texture, color, and shape. Whether it’s a couture dress or a cherished everyday item, our expert care helps extend the life of your garments while maintaining their elegance and integrity.

Sustainable Practices in Luxury Laundry

At the core of our luxury laundry service is a strong commitment to sustainability. We use environmentally friendly cleaning agents that are biodegradable, non-toxic, and free from harsh chemicals—ensuring garments are treated with care while minimizing environmental impact. Our energy-efficient machines, water-saving technologies, and reduced-plastic packaging reflect our ongoing efforts to protect the planet. With every garment we clean, we aim to deliver exceptional results without compromising the health of our environment or the quality our clients expect.

Professional Pressing

Our expert pressing service ensures every garment is finished to perfection. Using advanced steam and heat techniques, we smooth out every crease with precision, restoring the crisp structure and clean lines that elevate your appearance. Whether it's a tailored suit, delicate blouse, or formal evening wear, each item receives the utmost care to maintain its shape, fabric integrity, and polished look. Experience the difference of professionally pressed clothing — where flawless presentation meets refined attention to detail.
