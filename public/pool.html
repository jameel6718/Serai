Our Magnificent Swimming Pool

Situated on the ground floor, our sprawling 4400 square foot swimming pool offers a stunning retreat high above the city’s rhythm. Framed by tranquil architecture and panoramic views, it sets the stage for both rejuvenation and leisure. With temperature-controlled waters, elegant poolside loungers, and dedicated service, every detail is designed for pure indulgence. Whether you're taking a leisurely dip or sipping a cool beverage under the open sky, the experience is one of elevated relaxation.

Poolside Amenities

Relax in style at Serai’s poolside haven featuring luxurious private cabanas, comfortable sun loungers, and our popular swim-up bar. Sip on handcrafted cocktails, cool mocktails, or enjoy light snacks without ever leaving the water.

Swimming Lessons

We proudly offer swimming lessons tailored for all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced swimmers. Our certified instructors ensure a safe, encouraging environment where learners build confidence, improve technique, and enjoy the water.

Special Events

This summer, Serai’s swimming pool comes alive with exciting themed parties and exclusive events. From sunset DJ nights to fun-filled family splash days, each gathering promises music, refreshments, and unforgettable moments by the water.

Health & Safety

Your well-being is our top priority. At Serai, we follow strict hygiene and safety protocols, including regular sanitization, water quality checks, and trained lifeguard supervision — ensuring a safe and worry-free experience for every guest.

Pool Hours

Our swimming pool is open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Whether you prefer an energizing morning swim or a peaceful evening dip, enjoy the water at your convenience in a serene and luxurious setting.

Guest Feedback

At Serai, we truly value your feedback and believe it plays a vital role in helping us grow. Whether it’s a memorable moment, a helpful suggestion, or an area we can improve, your voice matters. Share your experiences with us.

Family Pool Area

T The family-friendly pool area is thoughtfully designed for both fun and safety, featuring a gradual entry ranging from 0.5m to 1.2m, making it ideal for young or beginner swimmers. Kids can enjoy interactive water jets, while shaded shallow zones provide a comfortable space for parents to relax nearby. With certified lifeguards on duty during operational hours, non-slip surfaces throughout, and complimentary swimming aids available, safety and comfort are always a priority. For added convenience, an adjacent poolside food and beverage service ensures everything you need is within easy reach.

Infinity Pool with Ocean View

Our signature 35-meter infinity pool offers a luxurious swimming experience with breathtaking panoramic views of the ocean horizon, seamlessly blending into the natural landscape. Designed for both relaxation and fitness, it features a temperature-controlled environment maintained at 28°C year-round, a shallow lounging area with submerged sunbeds, and a separate lap swimming zone for more active guests. The saltwater filtration system ensures a gentle, skin-friendly swim, while the pool remains open daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, allowing guests to unwind at sunrise or under the stars.
